PCRF Palestine Children's Relief Fund

Founded in 1991 by concerned humanitarians in the USA, PCRF provides free medical care to thousands of injured and ill children yearly who lack local access to care within the local health care system.

Close to 19,000 Gazan children have been killed. They have been the largest cohort of children amputees in modern history. Some had limbs amputated without anesthesia. There has been a new acronym created; WCNSF (wounded Child, no surviving family).
