You can easily find your representative by putting your ZIP code into this website.

Call Your Representative

You will speak to an intern/staff member. Be kind and respectful. Their job is to listen and take notes and log your message. If it feels meaningless, calls absolutely matter, especially if many people call about the same issue.

Below are also some sample scripts you can use to get some ideas. Feel free to adapt them to what you would want to say.

Write a Letter

A letter to their office is the most impactful form of contact, this would be the best option to sway our Utah representatives. Please always include your return address and ask for a response on their stance and how they intend to act.
