Template

Hi President Reese,

I urge the university to take meaningful action in response to the ongoing humanitarian crisis in Gaza and BYU’s complicity through silence and investments. In alignment with the moral values BYU seeks to uphold, I respectfully request the following:

  1. Transparency: BYU must disclose its financial investments to allow for public accountability and ensure our resources are not used to support systems of oppression.
  2. Divestment: BYU must divest from:
    • Israeli companies
    • Corporations materially supporting Israeli occupation and apartheid, such as Intel, Google, and AirBnB;
    • Weapons manufacturers supplying the Israeli military, including Boeing, Northrop Grumman, Lockheed Martin, and RTX.These demands reflect those raised by students across the country, including at Columbia, Cornell, and the University of Utah.
  3. Jerusalem Center Accountability: BYU must acknowledge that the BYU Jerusalem Center sits on land taken from Palestinians. This includes:
    • Canceling its Study Abroad and Travel Study programs to the Center indefinitely, as it did from 2001–2006;
    • Either terminating its lease with the Israel Land Authority early, or refusing to renew it upon expiration in 2037.
  4. Moral Leadership: BYU must end its silence and release a public statement calling for an immediate and permanent ceasefire in Gaza, and urge government officials to do the same.

Please respond as I’d like to know your position and how you plan to act. My true hope is that BYU will lead with integrity and compassion.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
