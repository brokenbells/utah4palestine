Call Our Senators

You will speak to an intern/staff member. Be kind and respectful. Their job is to listen and take notes and log your message. If it feels meaningless, calls absolutely matter, especially if many people call about the same issue.

Below are also some sample scripts you can use to get some ideas. Feel free to adapt them to what you would want to say.

Script 1: U.S. Funding & Ceasefire

Hello, my name is [Your Name], and I’m a constituent from [City], Utah.

I’m calling to urge Senator [Lee/Curtis] to take immediate action to end U.S. military aid to Israel and call for an immediate ceasefire in Gaza.

  • The U.S. gives $3.8 billion in military aid to Israel every year — that’s about $25 per taxpayer, and it’s directly funding the destruction in Gaza.
  • Since October 2023, over 30,000 Palestinians have been killed, many of them women and children.
  • Our government should not be funding bombs while people in Gaza are starving, dehydrated, and denied medical care.
  • I’m asking the Senator to push for a ceasefire, the safe entry of humanitarian aid, and an end to all military funding to Israel until civilian lives are protected.

Please let me know where the Senator stands on this. Thank you.

Script 2: Stop Aiding Genocide

Hi, my name is [Your Name], and I live in [City], Utah.

I’m calling because I am heartbroken and outraged by what’s happening in Gaza — and I want Senator [Lee/Curtis] to help stop the killing.

I cannot believe our country is still sending bombs and money while thousands of innocent people are being slaughtered. Mothers are digging their children out of rubble. Hospitals are bombed. People are starving — and we’re funding it.

This is not self-defense. It’s a massacre. And it’s happening with our support.

I’m asking Senator [Lee/Curtis] to:

  • Demand a ceasefire.
  • End U.S. military aid to Israel.
  • Prioritize getting humanitarian aid into Gaza now.

Please tell the Senator that Utahns are watching. We do not want genocide done in our name. I would like to know what he is doing to stop this.

Thank you.
